S-01 Official Mandate

Objective

The Ministry of Groove (Pty) Ltd is established as the primary custodian of high-fidelity sound and curated social architecture within the Mpumalanga region.

The Sonic Standard

The Ministry rejects the saturation of commercial noise. We are committed to the preservation of Deep House, Soulful House, and Rare Grooves.

The Delegate Protocol

Admission to our Sessions is governed by the Registry to ensure the integrity of our atmosphere remains uncompromised. By Invitation Only.
